Payment Plan With Control-a-bill

We know that changing seasons and other factors can mean that your energy usage can be a bit all over the place. That's why we've come up with Control-a-bill, a convenient way to pay that lets you spread your energy payments out evenly, so you pay the same amount on each bill, helping you budget better and take control of your energy payments.
