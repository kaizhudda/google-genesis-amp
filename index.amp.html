Pay your bill

Pay your power bills online whenever and wherever you like. Ensure you receive your prompt payment discount by paying on time.

Pay Via My Accountv

View and pay your power bill using Credit/Debit Card, Direct Debit or Automatic Payment via My Account.

Pay via Mobile Appv

View and pay your power bill using your Credit/Debit Card via the new Genesis Energy Mobile App

Payment Plan With Control-a-billv

We know that changing seasons and other factors can mean that your energy usage can be a bit all over the place.

That's why we've come up with Control-a-bill, a convenient way to pay that lets you spread your energy payments out evenly, so you pay the same amount on each bill, helping you budget better and take control of your energy payments.
